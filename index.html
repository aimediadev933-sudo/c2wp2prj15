Project Overview

Objective

To provide technical training on wireless networking and security management to engineers and academic IT staff to enhance campus wireless infrastructure and ensure secure, stable connectivity across National Research and Education Networks in the region.

Focus Areas

  • Wireless network deployment and planning
  • Network troubleshooting techniques
  • Security management best practices
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Issue resolution methodologies
  • Regional collaboration frameworks

Activities Conducted

Training Components

  • 4-day hands-on workshop with theory and lab sessions
  • Wireless network installation and configuration
  • Network monitoring and security services
  • Practical troubleshooting exercises
  • Peer-to-peer learning sessions

Additional Activities

  • Opening and closing ceremonies
  • Cultural night and networking events
  • Site visits to local facilities
  • Social dinner for relationship building
  • Formation of online collaboration groups
  • Creation of promotional materials and documentation

Key Outcomes & Impact

Immediate Results

  • 38 participants successfully trained (35 men, 3 women)
  • Strong knowledge gain reported by participants
  • Enhanced technical capabilities across institutions
  • Established feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement

Long-term Impact

  • Network improvements implemented at home institutions
  • Strengthened institutional collaboration networks
  • Replication of learning within participant organizations
  • Enhanced regional digital infrastructure capacity
  • Improved campus wireless connectivity and security

Challenges & Future Directions

Challenges Identified

  • Visa and travel complications limited full participation
  • Scheduling conflicts reduced country representation
  • Nomination processes created participation barriers
  • Evolving security threats require continuous updates
  • Geopolitical and environmental factors affecting planning

Future Directions

  • Annual workshops on evolving wireless technologies
  • Strengthened remote support platforms for NRENs
  • Follow-up site visits for hands-on deployment mentoring
  • Creation of permanent learning modules
  • Development of open-access technical repositories
  • Enhanced remote support channels
  • Strategic alignment with SDG implementation frameworks
